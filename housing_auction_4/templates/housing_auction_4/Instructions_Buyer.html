Experiment Instructions

Thank you for participating! You have earned $5 for showing up on time. The instructions explain how you can make decisions and earn more money, so please read carefully. Talking with other participants or use of cell phones is not permitted during the course of this experiment. If you have questions at any point, raise your hand and an experimenter will will come and assist you.

You have been assigned the role buyer and will be paired randomly with a group of other participants. Your identity in this group will remain anonymous , and likewise, the identities of all other group members will remain anonymous as well. There will be members in your group who are assigned to the role of buyer and other members who are assigned to the role of seller. All group membership and assigned roles will remain fixed during the course of the experiment.

Experimental Points

During the experiment you have the opportunity to earn experimental dollars (E$). There is a conversion ratio of {{Constants.conversion_rate}} E$ to 1 US$. At the end of the experiment, your earnings in experimental dollars will be converted and paid out to you in US$.

Overview

In this experiment, each seller is assigned an object that they will put up for sale in an auction. In the first stage, each seller will set a minimum price they will ask for their object. If the highest bid for the object does not exceed the minimum asking price, then the object will not be sold. However, if the highest bid does exceed the minimum asking price, then the object will be sold to the bidder with the highest bid.

In the second stage, you will have an opportunity to bid for the objects. You will be presented with a table that will provide you information about the object. An example of what you might see is displayed below.

For each object, you will be assigned a reservation value for that object. This represents how many experimental dollars you would earn if you were able to obtain the ojbect for a bid of 0 E$. These reservation values will be drawn uniformly from the integer range 1 to 10. This means that each value, 1 to 10, is equally likely. Each of the other buyers and sellers will possess their own unique reservation values for each of the possible objects that are on sale in the auction, and these valuations are independent. This means that your specific reservation values will not give you any information about the reservation values of other players.

This two stage game will be repeated for a total of {{Constants.num_rounds}} rounds. In each round, you and all other players will retain the same reservation values for each object that you did in the first round. However, sellers may update their ask prices and buyers may submit new bids. At the end of each round you will be presented with your earnings for the round. At the end of {{Constants.num_rounds}} rounds, you will recieve your final payout for the experiment which is the sum of all your earnings in each of the previous rounds.

Your Decision

In the auction stage, you can submit bids for any of the objects listed. This period will last until all player have clicked the button to end the round. If at the end of the period, you have the highest bid for an object, and this bid is higher than the minimum ask price set for this object, then you will purchase this object at the price of your bid. In order to submit a bid for an object, your bid must be higher than the current standing bid. Additionally, you may not submit a bid that is higher than your reservation value for the object. If at any time you have the standing bid for an object, this will be noted on the top of your screen and you will not be able to submit bids for any other objects.

Payoff

After the auction stage, your payout will be calculated and displayed to you. If you did not purchase an object in the auction stage, then your earnings for that round will be 0 E$. However, if you did have the standing bid for an object at the end of the auction stage, then your payoff for the round will be determined by the following formula,

Payout = Reservation Value - Bid

For instance, if your reservation value is 7 E$ and your bid is 4 E$, then your payout for that round will be 3 E$.

End of Experiment

At the end of the experiment you will be taken to a screen that displays your earnings in each round that you played, as well as the sum of earnings across each of the rounds, and that amount converted into US$. Please wait in your seat once you reach this screen. The experimenter will call you in one at a time to be paid out privately. Since you will be asked to leave when finished, please take all of your belongings up with you when you are called
