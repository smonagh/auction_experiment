Experiment Instructions

Thank you for participating! You have earned $5 for showing up on time. The instructions explain how you can make decisions and earn more money, so please read carefully. Talking with other participants or use of cell phones is not permitted during the course of this experiment. If you have questions at any point, raise your hand and an experimenter will will come and assist you.

You have been assigned the role seller and will be paired randomly with a group of other participants. Your identity in this group will remain anonymous , and likewise, the identities of all other group members will remain anonymous as well. There will be members in your group who are assigned to the role of buyer and other members who are assigned to the role of seller. All group membership and assigned roles will remain fixed during the course of the experiment.

Experimental Points

During the experiment you have the opportunity to earn experimental dollars (E$). At the end of the experiment, your earnings will be converted at ratio {{Constants.conversion_rate}} E$ to 1 US$ and paid out to you in US$.

Overview

In this experiment you are a seller. Your goal is to sell one widget to a buyer. The widget you sell is worth money at the end of the experiment. The total amount you earn in this experiment is equal to the total value of the widget you sold minus your money value for that widget.

In the first stage of the experiment you and each of the other sellers will post their desired price for their widget. This value can be set between 1 and 40 on a slider. When setting your desired price, you will see the following slider,

{% formfield player.ask_price with label="Please submit an ask price? " %}

Then, in the second stage, you will try and sell your widget to buyers. The buyers with the highest bid will purchase your widget, as long as bid submitted by this buyer exceeds the price that you posted. When buyers are bidding, you will see a table with information about the widgets. An example of what you might see is displayed below.

Current Bid

You can exchange the widget you sell to buyers for money at the end of the experiment. The widget assigned to you has a randomly determined money value of between 1 and 40 E$, and each of these values is equally likely. The same widget might have a different value for different buyers. That is, your value for a widget is not necessarily the same as the buyer’s value for your widget. Likewise, each seller has a different money value for their own widget. The value of your widget does not factor into your earnings. Therefore, you cannot earn money if you do not sell your widget.

Today’s experiment will include N rounds. Your value for your widget will always be the same, but you will be able to change your ask, and buyers will be able to change their bids, within each new round. At the end of each round you will be told your earnings for that round. However, note that only one round will be randomly chosen to be paid at the end of the experiment.

Your Decision

In the first stage, you will be asked to set a minimum asking price for your object. This is the lowest price at which bidders will be able to purchase your widget from you. You will be asked to set this price between 1 and 40 E$ on a slider. Once you have set the price that you like, then click on the Next button to submit your ask price. Note the ask price that you set must greater than or equal to your value for the widget. If it is not, then the ask price for your widget will be reset to the value of the widget. After each of the sellers has submitted their ask prices, all players will then proceed to the auction stage.

Payoff

After the auction stage, your payout will be calculated and displayed to you. If you did not sell your widget in the auction stage, then your earnings for that round will be 0 E$. However, if you did sell your widget, then your payoff for the round will be determined by the following formula,

Payout = Bid - Widget's Value

For instance, if your money value for your widget is 3 E$ and the bid for your widget is 7 E$, then your payout for that round will be 4 E$.

End of Experiment

At the end of the experiment you will be taken to a screen that displays your E$ earnings in each round, the round that was randomly chosen as the payoff round, and the earnings from that round converted into US$. Please wait in your seat once you reach this screen. An experimenter will call you in one at a time to be paid privately. When called, please take all your belongings up since the experiment ends once you have received your payment.
