INFORMED CONSENT FORM

RESEARCH PROCEDURES

This research is being conducted to study decision making. The experiment will last approximately one and half hours (90 minutes). In addition to $10 for showing up on time and participating, you may earn a positive amount of approximately $10 on average, but this amount will vary considerably based on your decisions, chance, and the decisions of others. Participants who do not arrive to the web page on time are not eligible for participation or the show-up fee. You will be paid at the end of the experiment through a deposit to your Venmo account. You are free to ask any questions about how you will be paid (please see the contact information below). Additional earnings will only be paid upon completion of the whole experiment. If you withdraw early, you will keep the $10 show-up fee. If you agree, you will be asked to make decisions in a few moments.

RISKS

There are no foreseeable risks for participating in this research.

BENEFITS

There are no direct personal benefits to you as a participant.

CONFIDENTIALITY

The data in this study will be confidential. All electronic files will be saved confidentially on a password-protected Laptop and in the ICES (Interdisciplinary Center for Economic Science) password-protected and access-restricted local area network. No person-identifiable information will be reported in any published or unpublished work. The data is protected in the local area network as secured by the GMU Netware log in procedures. Access is restricted to the professors and researchers associated with ICES. While it is understood that no computer transmission can be perfectly secure, reasonable efforts will be made to protect the confidentiality of your transmission. Participants may review Google's website for information about their privacy statement regarding Google Forms: https://policies.google.com/privacy. Participants may also review Zoom's website for information about their privacy statement: https://zoom.us/privacy. Identifiers may be removed from the data and the de-identified data could be used for future research without additional consent from participants.

PARTICIPATION

Your participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw from the study at any time and for any reason. To participate in this experiment, you must be 18 years or older. If you decide not to participate or if you withdraw from the study, there is no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

CONTACT

This research is being conducted by Cesar Martinelli and Artur Dolgopolov at George Mason University. They may be reached at 703-993-4853, 703-479-8700 for questions or to report a research-related problem. You may contact the George Mason University Institutional Review Board (IRB) office at 703-993-4121 if you have questions or comments regarding your rights as a participant in the research (IRBNet reference number: 1382818-1). This research has been reviewed according to George Mason University procedures governing your participation in this research. The George Mason University Institutional Review Board has waived the requirement for a signature on this consent form.
